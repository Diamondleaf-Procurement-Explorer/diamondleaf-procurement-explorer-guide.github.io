Diamondleaf Public Procurement Explorer

This page provides orientation and guidance for understanding the analytics presented in the Diamondleaf Public Procurement Explorer.

What this explorer shows

The Explorer aggregates and visualizes publicly available European public procurement notices, enabling structured analysis across countries, sectors, and procurement procedures.

The data reflects what has been published in official procurement systems. It does not represent real-time contract execution or financial settlement.

How to read the analytics

Procurement processes consist of multiple notice types (e.g. planning, contract award, modification). Dashboards may therefore reflect different stages of the same procurement lifecycle.

Aggregated figures should be interpreted as indicators of activity and structure, not as exact financial totals.

Navigation overview

  • Use filters to scope results by country, sector, or procedure
  • Dashboards are grouped by notice and form type
  • Hover and drill-down features reveal additional context

Limitations & caveats

Public procurement data is subject to reporting practices, legal constraints, and publication delays. Differences between dashboards may reflect regulatory structure rather than inconsistencies.
